QA Tester

Dealer Webb is searching for a Quality Assurance Tester to join our team! This position requires thorough understanding of computer programming concepts and a strong interest in technology and its advancement. Qualified candidates will test new features and functionality with a strong understanding of Group O’s web-based and internal systems developed to support Fortune 500 clients. Effective oral communication skills conveying ideas using both technical and nontechnical terms based on audience are vital. Strong written communication and documentation skills required; accurately documenting test procedures and results allowing for continuous process improvement and institutionalizing product knowledge. Candidate must provide dedication to provide a high level of customer service, striving for excellence and astute sensitivity to detail.

Job Description:

  • Approach system testing with professionalism and enthusiasm
  • Quickly learn new and complex systems
  • Evaluate and test new or modified systems to verify that functionality meets stated user requirements
  • Plan, design, and author effective test cases meeting business requirements
  • Adapt to new testing software/Application Lifecycle Management (ALM) software and other testing tools
  • Test implementations/integration with third party platforms
